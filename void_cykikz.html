About
JUST A
PERSON WHO
LIKES 3D
Hey, I'm Cykikz. This is my personal 3D viewer — a tool I built to drop models in and mess around with lighting without spinning up Blender every time.

I'm into 3D art, real-time rendering, and seeing how light behaves on different materials. No studio, no agency — just someone who finds this stuff genuinely interesting and wanted a clean place to experiment.

Drop any .GLB or .GLTF file onto the viewport above, tweak the lights, and see what it looks like. That's the whole point.

How It Works
1 Drag & drop any .GLB or .GLTF onto the viewport
2 Orbit with drag, zoom with scroll, pan with right-drag
3 Pick a light preset or fine-tune with the panel (bottom right)
4 Dark models? Crank ambient & exposure — they'll show up
Built with Three.js r128, GLTFLoader, OrbitControls, PMREMGenerator env maps, ACES filmic tone mapping, and shadow maps. No backend. Fully local — your files never leave the browser.
